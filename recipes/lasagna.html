Lasagna Recipe

A F*** LasagnYEET

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add marinara sauce to the meat mixture and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and cheese on top.
  7. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
