Syrup

  • 2 cups pure maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  1. Pour the maple syrup and water into a medium saucepan.
  2. Bring to a gentle simmer over medium heat.
  3. Stir occasionally and let simmer for 5â€“10 minutes to slightly thicken.
  4. If desired, stir in salt and vanilla extract for extra flavor.
  5. Remove from heat and let cool slightly before serving.
  6. Serve warm over pancakes, waffles, or your favorite breakfast foods.
  7. Store leftover syrup in a sealed container in the refrigerator.
